ہماری کہانی - Our Story

Janooon Café was born from a little dream that drifted between the streets of Islamabad and the spirit of Brooklyn—a craving for chai that felt like home, snacks that sparked nostalgia, and a space where people could slow down and connect. Our family-run café blends South Asian street favorites with modern café classics—like spiced lattes, minty mojitos, and bold gola gandas—served in a cozy, playful space that invites you to stay awhile. "Janooon" means passion—and it's at the heart of every flavor, every detail, and every smile we share.

"Janooon hai toh raaste khud ban jaate hain,

Warna irade bhi kabhi kabhi thak jaate hain."
